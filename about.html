About KK Computers

Empowering careers through excellence in IT education

Our Story

Founded in 2010, KK Computers has been at the forefront of IT education, providing comprehensive training programs that bridge the gap between academic knowledge and industry requirements. Our journey began with a simple mission: to empower individuals with the technological skills needed to thrive in the digital age.

Over the years, we have evolved from a small training center to a premier IT education institute, serving over 1,500 students and maintaining partnerships with leading technology companies. Our commitment to excellence and innovation has made us a trusted name in the IT training industry.

Today, we continue to adapt and expand our offerings to meet the ever-changing demands of the technology sector, ensuring our students are always equipped with the most current and relevant skills.
